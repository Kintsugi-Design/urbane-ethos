Resize the viewport below 768px. Then:

  1. Click .nav-toggle → panel opens, aria-expanded="true", first link focused, icon morphs to X.
  2. Tab forward — focus cycles within the panel (after the last link, wraps to the first).
  3. Shift+Tab from first link → wraps to last.
  4. Press Escape → panel closes, focus returns to toggle.
  5. Open again, click outside the panel → closes (no focus restore).
  6. Open, then widen viewport > 768px → panel reverts to horizontal nav; toggle hides.
